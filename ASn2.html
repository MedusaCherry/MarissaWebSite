Asteroids '79 Redesign

Modern technology allows us to expand on game ideas in ways that
the creaters on the games could have only imagined. Not only has
computing power increased exponentially since Asteroids was created,
new sensors and equipment have been invented to make user experiences
more immersive. Nowadays it in not just science fiction to create an
Asteriods game in VR where each asteroid is a planet with its own randomly
generated ecosystem with A.I. capable of evovling over time in a simulated
universe with concievably infinite possible experiences for the user to stumble upon.
